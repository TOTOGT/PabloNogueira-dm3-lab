## Supplement: Collatz as Corollary of Crystal Geometry (Grossi 2026)

Reference: Pablo Nogueira Grossi, *The Collatz Conjecture as a Corollary of Crystal Geometry*, Supplement to Principia Orthogona Book III. Zenodo DOI: [10.5281/zenodo.19378742](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.19378742).

Summary: The supplement argues that the Collatz map is visible as an instance of the dm3/G6 crystal geometry; it frames c=3 as a triad fingerprint and identifies the remaining gap as a formal-language extension (AXLE Target 5). See the Zenodo record for the full PDF.
